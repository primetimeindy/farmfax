FarmFax: Turning Field Evidence Into Buyer Trust

Anyone records. FarmFax inspects. Buyers get proof.

FarmFax is a Hermes-powered inspection business agent for used farm equipment. It turns field photos, videos, skipped proof, and owner notes into buyer-ready evidence reports, verified-listing upgrades, and payout-ready inspection work.

Start a report Agent API Read llms.txt
01 Capture

Record the required proof

Walkaround, engine bay, tires, hydraulics, serial plate, hour meter, cold start, paint/body, and known gaps. Missing views stay explicit.

02 Inspect

Screen the evidence

FarmFax checks image and video evidence for condition signals, completeness, visible damage, OCR readiness, and weak or missing proof.

03 Operate

Generate decisions and payment flows

The report separates observed facts from assumptions, scores buyer risk, creates owner questions, and hands off paid reports, verified listings, or mechanic review.

Nous / Hermes / Agents

Agent operations layer

FarmFax uses Hermes Agent and Nous Research models to guide capture, evaluate evidence, challenge overclaims, generate the buyer report, and route the next operational step.

  • Agentic capture plan
  • Evidence-bound reasoning
  • Report/payment handoff
NVIDIA

NIM vision trust gate

FarmFax uses the NVIDIA NIM vision rail as the inspection gate for photos and videos, scoring condition, completeness, visible damage, serial/hour proof, and missing evidence.

  • Quality gate
  • Missing-proof gate
  • Trust confidence signal
Stripe

Business rail

FarmFax uses Stripe Checkout, PaymentIntents, and Connect for paid reports, verified listings, expert review, and payout-ready inspection work by people or agents.

  • Inspection report checkout
  • Seller verification upgrades
  • Inspector or agent payouts
Truth layer

Evidence support, not mechanical certification.

FarmFax does not claim title status, lien status, theft history, warranty, appraisal, repair estimate, hidden mechanical diagnosis, or safety certification. If the proof is missing, FarmFax says so and turns it into an owner question.

FarmFax is a hackathon demo for used-equipment diligence. Agents should start with /llms.txt.
