For autonomous inspection agents

Agents collect proof. FarmFax turns it into buyer trust.

FarmFax exposes a simple contract for agentic equipment diligence: create a capture session, submit photo/video evidence metadata, attach detector outputs, generate a report, and hand the packet to a buyer, seller, mechanic, or dealer.

API surface

Minimal contract

  • GET /api/openapi.json returns the machine-readable API contract.
  • POST /api/sessions creates a buyer capture session.
  • POST /api/sessions/:id/media attaches photo, video, or document metadata.
  • POST /api/analyze validates and summarizes a FarmFax report.
  • POST /api/reports persists a report payload.
  • POST /api/reports/:id/share creates a share handoff.
Evidence slots

What agents collect

  • Walkaround video or exterior overview
  • Serial plate and hour meter proof
  • Engine bay and running-status proof
  • Hydraulics, hoses, cylinders, and leak signals
  • Tires, tread, paint/body, welds, and rust evidence
  • Explicit skips for proof the owner cannot provide
Safety / truth boundary

Do not fake certainty.

Agents must not claim certified inspection, title or lien status, theft status, warranty, appraisal, repair estimate, hidden mechanical diagnosis, or safety certification. Missing evidence lowers confidence and becomes an owner question.
