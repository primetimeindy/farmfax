# Commercial Autonomy Proof v0.1

Revenue Forge introduces a sponsor-native proof layer for autonomous business agents.

## Purpose
Prove that a commercial agent did not merely generate content. It operated under a governed chain of custody:

1. Hermes orchestrated agent tasks and skills.
2. NVIDIA-class runtime executed/scaled the workloads and policy checks.
3. Stripe provided the commerce/spend/monetization rail.
4. Revenue Forge recorded every action as an auditable proof packet.

## Core event types

- `mission.created`
- `market.scored`
- `asset.generated`
- `spend.warrant.requested`
- `operator.approval.signed`
- `stripe.checkout.created`
- `ledger.verdict.issued`

## Required proof fields

- `packet_id`
- `standard`
- `mission.objective`
- `mission.budget_cap_usd`
- `mission.live_charges_enabled`
- `sponsor_stack[]`
- `events[]`
- `ledger[]`

- **Nous Research:** Hermes becomes the operating system for commercial agents.
- **Stripe:** Stripe becomes the financial control plane for agentic businesses.
- **NVIDIA:** NVIDIA becomes the governed accelerated runtime for real business workloads.
