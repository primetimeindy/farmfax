# Revenue Forge — Hackathon Submission Brief

## Submission title
Revenue Forge: Commercial Autonomy Proof for Agentic Revenue Operations

## Short description
Revenue Forge is a supervised agentic revenue machine. A founder gives it a commercial objective and a budget cap; Hermes agents research the market, forge the offer, request spend authority, provision a Stripe test checkout, and stamp every step into an auditable proof packet.

## Problem
Business agents are crossing from chat into real operations. Once agents can spend money, create checkout rails, buy tools, or contact customers, founders and enterprises need proof:

- Who authorized the action?
- What policy constrained it?
- What money could it spend?
- What payment rail did it create?
- What runtime executed the work?
- What outcome did it produce?
- Should the experiment scale, hold, or die?

Most AI products generate content. They do not prove commercial accountability.

## Solution
Revenue Forge introduces **Commercial Autonomy Proof**: a sponsor-native chain of custody for agentic business operations.

The demo shows a single revenue mission transformed into a governed experiment:

1. PRIME decomposes the objective.
2. SCOUT scores reachable demand.
3. GROWTH forges offer assets.
4. OPS requests a Spend Warrant and blocks chargeable actions.
5. The operator signs limited spend authority.
6. RAILS opens a Stripe test payment rail.
7. LEDGER issues a scale/hold/kill verdict.
8. The app exports a JSON/Markdown Proof Packet.

## Why it matters for Nous Research
Revenue Forge shows Hermes Agent as an operating system for commercial agents, not a chatbot. The visible Agent Circuit and proof events turn skills, tool calls, approvals, and logs into a durable business workflow.

## Why it matters for Stripe
Stripe becomes the commerce control plane for autonomous businesses: spend warrants, checkout sessions, price rails, live-charge locks, and attribution all become visible proof objects.

## Why it matters for NVIDIA
Commercial agents need governed, accelerated runtime infrastructure. Revenue Forge frames research scoring, asset generation, policy checks, and ROI decisions as workloads that scale from local execution to NVIDIA-class runtime.

## Moat
The moat is not copy generation. It is a closed-loop dataset and proof layer connecting:

ICP hypothesis → offer → spend authority → payment rail → revenue signal → ROI verdict.

Over time, the proprietary data is which commercial hypotheses produced money under what constraints.

## Demo path
- Load objective.
- Forge artifacts through PRIME, SCOUT, GROWTH, OPS.
- Stop at Spend Warrant.
- Sign limited authority.
- Raise Stripe rail receipt.
- Show Commercial Autonomy Proof Ledger.
- Export Proof Packet.

Hermes orchestrates. NVIDIA accelerates. Stripe settles. Revenue Forge proves every dollar and decision.
