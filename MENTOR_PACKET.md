# Revenue Forge — Mentor Packet

## One-line thesis
Revenue Forge is a Hermes-powered prototype for governed agentic commerce: **Hermes orchestrates, NVIDIA accelerates, Stripe settles, and Revenue Forge proves every dollar and decision.**

## What it is
Revenue Forge turns a founder’s revenue objective, budget cap, and constraints into a supervised commercial experiment. Instead of merely generating copy, Hermes agents coordinate a workflow that researches demand, forges an offer, requests spend authority, opens a Stripe test payment rail, and exports a proof packet showing what happened and why.

## The problem
As agents move from chat into real business operations, they will start taking actions that matter: spending money, creating checkout pages, contacting customers, buying tools, and launching experiments. Businesses need a way to prove who authorized each action, what budget constrained it, what payment rail was created, and whether the experiment should scale, hold, or stop.

## The solution
Revenue Forge introduces **Commercial Autonomy Proof**: an auditable chain of custody for agentic business operations. The key primitive is the **Spend Warrant** — when money is involved, the agent physically stops and requires scoped human approval before proceeding.

## Demo workflow
1. **PRIME** decomposes the founder’s objective.
2. **SCOUT** scores reachable market demand.
3. **GROWTH** creates offer assets.
4. **OPS** requests a Spend Warrant.
5. The operator signs limited authority.
6. **RAILS** opens a Stripe test checkout rail.
7. **LEDGER** issues a scale / hold / kill verdict.
8. The system exports a JSON/Markdown Proof Packet.

## Why it matters
Revenue Forge frames Hermes as an operating system for accountable business agents, Stripe as the commerce control plane for agentic payments and approvals, and NVIDIA as the accelerated runtime path for commercial agent workloads.

## Strategic moat
The moat is not AI-generated marketing copy. It is the closed-loop proof layer connecting:

**ICP hypothesis → offer → spend authority → payment rail → revenue signal → ROI verdict**

Over time, this creates proprietary data about which commercial experiments produce revenue under which constraints.

## Current status
The prototype is built as a React/Vite app with an Agent Circuit UI, Revenue Packet, Spend Warrant flow, Stripe Rail Receipt mock, Commercial Autonomy Proof Ledger, and exportable proof packet. Next steps are deployment, demo video, and final hackathon submission packaging.
