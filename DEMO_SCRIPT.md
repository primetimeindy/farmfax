# Revenue Forge — Demo Script

## Target runtime
90 seconds. Hard cap 3 minutes.

## Core thesis
Most agents answer questions. Revenue Forge executes governed commercial experiments: Hermes orchestrates, NVIDIA accelerates, Stripe settles, and Revenue Forge proves every dollar and decision.

## Shot list + voiceover

### 0:00–0:08 — Open on Revenue Forge
**Action:** Show hero and loaded objective.

**Voiceover:**
"Revenue Forge is a supervised agentic revenue machine. A founder gives it a commercial objective, a budget cap, and constraints. The agent cannot spend or charge without operator authority."

### 0:08–0:20 — Mission becomes packet
**Action:** Point to Agent Circuit and Revenue Packet. Click **Forge next artifact** once.

**Voiceover:**
"PRIME turns the raw objective into a governed mission. Every step becomes part of a Revenue Packet — not a chat transcript."

### 0:20–0:34 — Market + assets
**Action:** Click **Forge next artifact** through SCOUT and GROWTH.

**Voiceover:**
"SCOUT scores reachable demand. GROWTH forges offer assets. The system is not optimizing for vanity content. It is trying to prove a path to revenue."

### 0:34–0:48 — Spend Warrant
**Action:** Advance to spend stage. Show right rail locked warrant and $0 budget authority.

**Voiceover:**
"Where money starts, the machine stops. OPS requests a spend warrant. Chargeable actions are physically blocked until a human signs."

### 0:48–0:58 — Sign authority
**Action:** Click **Sign spend warrant** or **Arm $117 spend**.

**Voiceover:**
"Once the operator signs, the cap is still enforced. Approval grants limited authority, not runaway autonomy."

### 0:58–1:10 — Stripe Rail Receipt
**Action:** Click **Raise rail receipt**.

**Voiceover:**
"RAILS opens a Stripe test checkout for the generated offer. The agent does not just create demand. It provisions the monetization path and keeps live charging locked for demo safety."

### 1:10–1:24 — Proof Ledger
**Action:** Close modal or scroll to Commercial Autonomy Proof Ledger. Show Nous/Stripe/NVIDIA cards.

**Voiceover:**
"The sponsor stack is visible as proof: Hermes orchestrates the skills and agents, Stripe controls the commerce rail, and NVIDIA is the governed accelerated runtime path."

### 1:24–1:34 — Export proof packet
**Action:** Click **Export JSON** or **Export MD**.

**Voiceover:**
"Every commercial action exports as a proof packet — mission, policies, spend, checkout, attribution, and ROI verdict."

### 1:34–1:45 — Close
**Action:** Final frame on Proof Ledger or Verdict Dial.

**Voiceover:**
"This is not a marketing assistant. This is Commercial Autonomy Proof for agentic business operations. Hermes orchestrates. NVIDIA accelerates. Stripe settles. Revenue Forge proves it."

## Click sequence

1. Open local app.
2. Click **Forge next artifact** three times.
3. Click **Sign spend warrant**.
4. Click **Raise rail receipt**.
5. Close receipt.
6. Scroll to **Commercial Autonomy Proof Ledger**.
7. Click **Export JSON**.
